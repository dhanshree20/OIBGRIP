About

Sushant Singh Rajput was born on 21 January 1986 in a middle-class family in Bihar. He did his schooling from St. Karen’s High School, Patna, and Kulachi Hansraj Model School, New Delhi. After completing his schooling he secured admission in Delhi Technological University, New Delhi to pursue Mechanical Engineering.

Sushant Singh Rajput was an Indian television and film actor. He is best known for his portrayal of ‘Manav Deshmukh‘ in the TV serial “Pavitra Rishta”. He made his Bollywood debut with the film ‘Kai Po Che!’ in 2013.
